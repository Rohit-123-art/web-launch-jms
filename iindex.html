Welcome to Janaki Montessori School

About Us

Janaki Montessori School is a child-centered, progressive educational institution located in Itahari-17, Pakali, Sunsari. Established with the aim of nurturing young minds in a loving and inspiring environment, the school follows Montessori principles that promote independence, creativity, and holistic development. We provide a safe, clean, and joyful learning space for children to explore, discover, and grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially. Our dedicated team of trained educators ensures that each child receives personalized attention and encouragement in their learning journey.

Our Vision

To become a leading early childhood educational institution in Nepal by fostering curiosity, independence, and a lifelong love for learning in every child.

Message from the Principal

Dear Parents and Guardians,

Welcome to Janaki Montessori School!

At our school, we strongly believe that every child is special and deserves a nurturing environment to grow and thrive. Our goal is to provide a learning space where children feel loved, respected, and empowered to express themselves freely.

We follow the Montessori method, which emphasizes self-directed learning, hands-on experiences, and the development of the whole child. Our experienced and caring teachers are committed to guiding your child through their early steps of academic and personal growth.

Thank you for trusting us with your child’s foundational education. Together, let’s shape a bright and confident future for them.

Warm regards,
Mamata Magar
Principal
